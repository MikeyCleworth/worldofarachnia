    • New World Tarantulas

    When Mr Columbus discovered the Americas (Northern, Southern and Central), he coined these newfoundlands as 'The New World'. Consequently, any tarantula indigeonous to these parts of the world are catagorised as New World species.

    Ideal for Beginners

    Due to their usually docile nature, tarantulas from the New World collective are often recommended to beginners as first spiders. New World tarantulas are far less likely to attack; making them easier to clean, feed and maintain.

    The Venom of a New World Species

    Unlike Old World tarantulas, New Worlds have a very mild venom. A lot of people that have reported being bitten by a New World tarantula describe the swelling and pain inflicted on a similar level to a bee sting. There's very little known about allergies and tarantula venom however, so it's always best to exercise caution.

    Temperament

    New World tarantulas typically lack the angry charateristics of their Old World cousins.Although highly advised against, if you wanted a tarantula to handle, for instance, then a New World species would really be your only option. The same can be said if you have children that will be playing an active part in any husbandry. New World's are your friends.

    Defence

    New World spiders would much prefer to flee than attack if provoked or threatened. Furthermore, rather than biting, their first go-to if unable to flee are urticating hairs. These these are barbed in design and lodge themselves into your skin. The tarantula uses these by kicking their rear legs rapidly, shooting a barrage of the hairs towards you. Being on the receiving end of this is no fun as it causes severe irritation, itchyness and a rash.

    • My Top 4 New World Tarantulas

    Acanthoscurria geniculata

    Commonly known as the "Brazilian Whiteknee", the A.gen is a a fiesty fellow. With voricious appitites, these spiders can reach up to 8.5 inches across a diagonal leg-span. These are proud spiders and love to be out and about, rarely hiding. With vivid cream stripes on a chocolate brown body make these a stunning display piece and a must for every collection.
